Lore: Brewmaster and blacksmith, Torgar invokes the flame of the forge and the power of hops. His blessings may burn or bless—but always come with a tankard.

The Story So Far

The Four Great Realms

Beneath a fractured sky lie four powers locked in fragile balance – the war‑scarred Duchy of Malric, the mist‑veiled Sisterhood of the Shroud, the arcane‑spined Tower‑Cities of Vel Thalor, and the lawless expanse known only as The Wastes.

Rise of Archduke Malric

In the north, Archduke Malric has declared himself Sovereign Regent. His ruthless enforcer Baron Redmar fans the flames of war, while the once‑loyal Captain Branth now defends the borderlands as a sellsword commander.

Faith & Fear

Wandering healer Sister Elenwen revives forgotten gods, shadowed by zealot Inquisitor Vale. Meanwhile the cryptic Madame Nyla murmurs prophecies that change with every gust of wind.

Orc Alliance & Forbidden Magic

Gruk the Unyielding seeks to unite the orc clans for survival, aided by scout Faelan Duskwatch and diplomat Lady Seraphine. Lurking beyond them all is High Arcanist Volmir, whose forbidden sorcery threatens to rend reality itself – and through every crack dances Jinx Whistletoe, laughing with secrets.
