The Winds of Ovener

A D&D Campaign

The year is 834 AE. The ancient continent of Ovener trembles beneath the weight of war and prophecy. Kingdoms once held together by oaths and steel now splinter beneath the banners of forgotten gods. Rumors speak of a crimson comet blazing across the sky — a harbinger of an age where magic and shadow clash.

Our story begins in the borderlands, where a ragtag fellowship of heroes — outcasts, heirs, and mercenaries alike — are drawn together by fate, gold, and grudges. From cursed ruins to high courts, from the depths of the Underdark to the skies above dragon-spired peaks, they must face eldritch horrors, corrupt empires, and choices that echo through time.

Will they rise as legends, or be forgotten as dust in the road?

World Lore

For millennia, Ovener was a land of balance — between arcane and divine, order and chaos, mortal and myth. The First Age saw the rise of the Aurelian Empire, a golden civilization that spread magic through leyline conduits. But power bred corruption, and the Cataclysm shattered the empire, unleashing rogue gods and magical storms. In the ages since, kingdoms have risen and fallen atop its bones. The Crimson Comet marks the fourth turning — a signal that Ovener stands once again at the edge of epochal change.

Behold the realm of Ovener. From the storm-lashed coastlines of Kharval to the sun-scorched peaks of Drak'Thul, the land is ancient and brimming with danger and prophecy.

Places of Interest

Virehall, the City of Broken Oaths

A haunted metropolis of gothic decay. Once a glorious capital, now it lies under eternal twilight, where ghostly banners still wave and betrayed spirits linger.

Bramblehollow

A fey-touched forest valley where time bends and mischievous spirits whisper riddles. Mortals who wander here may never return the same.

Emberdeep Forge

An ancient forge-city beneath the Ashen Chasm, where dwarves and fire elementals craft weapons from living magma. Rumors speak of a chained primordial below.

Iskar's Wake

A sunken temple visible only at low tide, its halls filled with coral, drowned relics, and echoes of the sea god’s dreams.

The Scar of Thal'Zar

A reality-warped canyon where ancient magics collided. Spells fizzle unpredictably and time falters. At its core, a pulsing crystal heart beats with lost power.
