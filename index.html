The Winds of Ovener

A D&D Campaign

The year is 834 AE. The ancient continent of Ovener trembles beneath the weight of war and prophecy. Kingdoms once held together by oaths and steel now splinter beneath the banners of forgotten gods. Rumors speak of a crimson comet blazing across the sky — a harbinger of an age where magic and shadow clash.

Our story begins in the borderlands, where a ragtag fellowship of heroes — outcasts, heirs, and mercenaries alike — are drawn together by fate, gold, and grudges. From cursed ruins to high courts, from the depths of the Underdark to the skies above dragon-spired peaks, they must face eldritch horrors, corrupt empires, and choices that echo through time.

Will they rise as legends, or be forgotten as dust in the road?
